Tanko

3D chamber flow simulator

Projects saved on this device. Presets load into the current project.

Chambers
Openings

Holes in a shared wall. Drag bottom/top in side view, width in top view. Top above the wall crest → cascade; below → submerged window.

Tubes

Pipes between any two chambers. Diameter sets flow; click the valve to open/close.

Inflows
Drains

Outlets to outside. Adjacent walls also spill automatically over the lower rim; exposed walls lose fluid to the wild.

Pumps

Transfer water between chambers at a fixed rate. Click +add then click the source chamber, then the target. Use a level mark to auto-stop/start.
